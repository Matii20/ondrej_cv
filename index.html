Bio

My name is Ondrej Ferianec, a designer from Slovakia, with a Master’s degree in Industrial Design from Lund University. Growing up in a family of engineers and doctors, I chose a creative profession unconventionally. Finding my identity as a creative individual with an interest in sustainability involved overcoming challenges, defying expectations, and seeking acceptance in my surroundings. Besides my parents, my biggest role models are my uncle Majko and his wife Ivanka.
